Affinity - Own your Liquidity

What is it?

Affinity is a set of smart contracts that enable teams to purchase liquidity pool tokens from their users.

Why?

When users hold the liquidity pool tokens for your defi project you run the risk of users fleeing the protocol during a market downturn and leaving your protocol without any liquidity. This project is designed to allow you to incentive users to sell their liquidity pool tokens to you.

How was it built?

  • Plain rust. No Anchor.
  • Pyth for price oracles.
  • Supports Raydium, Orca Legacy Pools, Saber, and Token Swap Program liquidity pool tokens.

How it works

A team that wants to purchase lp tokens will create a bond system with a funded treasury account. They will set the rate at which bonds can be sold and what percentage the reward should be. Users will then be able to buy bonds for lp tokens. Then exercise them as the bonds mature.
